Our Services

A structured, phase-based approach targeting the highest-impact areas of your operation — in the order that builds lasting results.

Phase 1

Human Resources, Training & Development

A comprehensive review of your HR infrastructure alongside the development of structured training programs — building the people systems your operation needs to hire well, develop consistently, and retain the team that drives your performance.

Phase 2

Financial Performance & Cost Control

A deep-dive into your food cost, recipes, labour budgeting, and scheduling — identifying gaps between actual and ideal, and putting actionable systems in place to tighten your margins without sacrificing quality or the guest experience.

Phase 3

Franchise Support & Brand Compliance

Franchisee onboarding, brand standards auditing, and multi-unit consistency programs — for franchisors building scalable systems and franchisees navigating them.

About

Matthew Coates

Director of Client Services, R.O.S.S. / A Division of MP3 Management Ltd.

Matthew Coates brings nearly 40 years of hands-on restaurant industry experience to every client engagement. His career spans virtually every role in the business, from the floor and the line to management, consulting, and executive leadership.

Matthew has operated, managed, trained, and consulted across a remarkable breadth of environments: high-volume independents, major national and international franchise systems, high-end fine dining, and large-scale multi-unit operations. He has built training programs from scratch, launched new concepts, turned around underperforming locations, and coached hundreds of restaurant professionals into more effective leaders.

There are very few challenges in this business Matthew hasn't faced firsthand. Very few he hasn't solved.
